Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Introduction

Vault Checker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Vault Checker is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.

Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to save your preferences, such as:

  • Music player volume settings
  • Music player mute/unmute state
  • Music player expanded/collapsed state

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services:

YouTube API

We embed YouTube videos for background music. When you interact with the music player, YouTube's terms of service and privacy policy apply. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

External Data Sources

We fetch game data from:

  • Warframe Wiki: For relic and item information
  • Warframe Market: For market pricing data

These requests are made directly from your browser and do not pass through our servers.

Three.js and CDN Services

We use Three.js library served via CDN (Content Delivery Network) for 3D graphics rendering. This may result in requests to third-party CDN servers.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. Any cookies set by third-party services (such as YouTube) are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not monitor or track your usage of the website.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, there is no personal data at risk. All preferences are stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our website does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The service is provided as-is without any data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
